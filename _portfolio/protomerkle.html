--- title: "Protomerkle" excerpt: "Build merkle trees onchain
" collection: portfolio --- Merkle trees are a common primitive for blockchain applications, but inside the execution environment they are most commonly used for verification only due to gas and complexity restrictions. Some projects have implemented append-only merkle trees onchain. Protomerkle is a solidity library to build and verify merkle trees onchain. It is general purpose, supports inheritance and is extremely optimized. A merkle-sum tree implementation is also included, which is very useful for Proof of Reserves.
